7-Day Sri Lanka Highlights Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Sri Lanka

  • Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB)
  • Transfer to Negombo coastal town
  • Check-in at hotel
  • Evening at leisure with optional beach walk

Day 2: Sigiriya & Dambulla

  • Early departure from Negombo
  • Climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress
  • Visit Dambulla Cave Temple
  • Overnight in Dambulla

Day 3: Kandy & Peradeniya

  • Travel to Kandy (2.5 hours)
  • Visit Temple of the Tooth
  • Explore Royal Botanic Gardens
  • Visit Sandagiri Maha Seya

Day 4: Nuwara Eliya

  • Visit Ramayana-linked sites
  • Seetha Amman Temple
  • Tea estate and factory tour
  • Gregory Lake stroll

Day 5: Ella & Southern Coast

  • Ravana Falls stop
  • Nine Arches Bridge
  • Ravana Cave history
  • Arrive at Weligama/Mirissa

Day 6: Southern Beaches

  • Weligama Beach relaxation
  • Mirissa Beach exploration
  • Sunset over Indian Ocean
  • Rumassala legend

Day 7: Departure

  • Visit Kelaniya Temple
  • Vibhishan's Palace
  • Transfer to airport
  • Farewell to Sri Lanka
